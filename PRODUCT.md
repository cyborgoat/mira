# Mira Product Context

Register: product

Mira is a private daily work workspace for tasks, notes, team visibility, and a personal LLM-maintained wiki. It is used by individuals and managers who need a compact place to capture source material, turn it into durable markdown knowledge, and ask follow-up questions without rebuilding context every time.

Primary users:
- Individual contributors maintaining personal work notes and source documents.
- Managers reviewing team work while keeping private knowledge separate per account.

Product principles:
- Daily-use screens should be fast, compact, and easy to scan.
- AI features should feel like tools for maintaining a working archive, not a chat novelty.
- Uploaded source documents are treated as durable raw material; generated wiki pages are readable outputs.
- Keep language direct, calm, and operational.
